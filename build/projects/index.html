What Makes a Good CUSD Project?

A Meaningful Student Experience

An ideal project for CUSD to tackle is complex and impactful. We choose thought provoking projects that will help the community the project is in and will hone the skills of our members. Projects that include a student travel component are always welcomed as they expose students to new cultures and engage the partner community.

A Reliable Stakeholder

Ideally we look for a stakeholder that has an existing, positive relationship with the community, and is in need of low cost services to bring the project to fruition.

The stakeholderâ€™s mission should be aligned with the tangible aims of CUSD, and they should know exactly what they need from CUSD, but be open to creativity and innovation.

Mostly, we look for stakeholders who are willing and able to serve as a mentor throughout the project.

Multifaceted Projects

Projects should be multifaceted and a community priority. We tackle projects that require a design-build are driven or positively impacted by holistic sustainability.
