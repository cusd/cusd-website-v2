Overlook Ridge Development

Net-zero housing for rural New York

Located in Little Falls, New York, the Overlook Ridge Development combines cutting-edge, sustainable living with world-class leadership education.

The Overlook Ridge Campus will be home to six newly developed townhomes that feature a variety of innovative green technologies, contributing to the campus’ zero-net energy status. It will be home to individuals looking for a change in the form of an innovative, uplifting and sustainable community.

The three aspects of the ORD team are Design, Energy, and Public Relations. The Design team works on creating the outer “shells” of the townhouses in the neighborhood. Their goal is to make the housing units both aesthetically pleasing and environmentally sustainable. Meanwhile, the Energy team focuses on coming up with the most effective and plausible sources of energy. Options include using solar-thermal power and rooftop gardens. Members of the Public Relations team work on engaging the community in the project and keeping potential buyers up to date on the neighborhood.

Number of housing units 6
Reduction in heating and cooling energy demand 50%
Hot water provided by in-home solar water heaters 90%
