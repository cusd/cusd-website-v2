    Voices of African Mothers


    VAM is a 501(c)3 that empowers women and children through providing them with the education to advocate for a peaceful African continent. VAM currently leads several microfinance and empowerment initiatives and oversees the successful John Williams Montessori School in Kumasi, Ghana and has plans to create a Montessori high school nearby. The goal of VAM Girlsâ€™ Academy (part of the larger VAM Village initiative) is to provide girls in the Volta region of Ghana and beyond with a nurturing, inspiring educational environment. Ecological stewardship fit naturally into the project as the location of VAM Village is situated on a beautiful, environmentally rich part of Ghana. The unmet need of investing in girls rising to be champions in environmentalism and peace has continued to inspire the partnership of VAM and CUSD.
